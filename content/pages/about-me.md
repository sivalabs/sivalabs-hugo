## K. Siva Prasad Reddy

I am a software engineer, architect, trainer, and technical content creator. I have been building software professionally since 2006, with most of my work centered around Java, Spring Boot, distributed systems, and backend engineering.

I enjoy taking complex technical ideas and explaining them through practical examples. That is the main reason behind SivaLabs: sharing tutorials, opinions, and learning resources that help developers build better applications with confidence.

## What I Work On

* Building scalable backend applications with **Java**, **Kotlin**, **Go**, **Spring Boot**, and **Spring Cloud**.
* Designing **microservices**, **modular monoliths**, REST APIs, and event-driven systems.
* Working with **Kafka**, **RabbitMQ**, **AWS SQS**, Docker, Kubernetes, and cloud-native development practices.
* Automating delivery and infrastructure using **Jenkins**, **Terraform**, **AWS CDK**, and **Pulumi**.
* Exploring AI-assisted development with tools, agents, **Spring AI**, and **LangChain4j**.

## Teaching And Writing

I write tutorials and articles for both beginners and experienced developers. I also create video tutorials and training material around Java, Spring Boot, microservices, testing, architecture, and developer productivity.

I have authored books and long-form technical material, and I regularly share practical lessons from real-world software development.
